About Health Watch

Health Watch is a warm, evidence-based health and fitness platform built to make expert-level health guidance accessible to everyone — from complete beginners to seasoned athletes.

Our Mission

We believe that evidence-based health information should be approachable, actionable, and free from sensationalism. Every article on Health Watch is grounded in peer-reviewed research and reviewed by qualified practitioners.

Editorial Policy

Our content follows a rigorous editorial process:

  • All articles are written by qualified authors with relevant credentials.
  • Medical and health claims are supported by at least one peer-reviewed source.
  • YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) content is reviewed by a licensed healthcare professional.
  • We update articles when new research emerges that changes our recommendations.
  • Affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed and do not influence editorial content.

Our Commitment to You

We will never recommend a product or service we haven't independently evaluated. Our interactive tools are built using validated formulas from established institutions (WHO, NSCA, ACSM). We are transparent about our limitations and always recommend consulting a qualified professional for personal health decisions.
