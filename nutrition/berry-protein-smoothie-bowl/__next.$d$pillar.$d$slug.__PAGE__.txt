e:T407,<h2>Rich in Micronutrients and Muscle-Building Protein</h2>
<p>Smoothie bowls are a delicious way to pack multiple servings of nutrient-dense fruits and seeds into one meal. By blending frozen mixed berries, you obtain a concentrated dose of <strong>polyphenols and vitamin C</strong>, which help reduce systemic inflammation.</p>
<h2>Crafting the Perfect Texture</h2>
<p>The secret to a thick, scoopable smoothie bowl is patience and a high-powered blender. Do not rush to add more liquid. Use a tamper to press the frozen ingredients into the blades until a thick "soft-serve" consistency is achieved.</p>
<h2>Customizing Your Toppings</h2>
<p>The toppings add texture and healthy fats to slow down digestion:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Chia seeds</strong>: Provide omega-3 fatty acids and soluble fiber.</li>
<li><strong>Granola</strong>: Adds crunch. Choose a grain-free or low-sugar option to avoid a blood sugar spike.</li>
<li><strong>Nut butter</strong>: A drizzle of almond or peanut butter adds monounsaturated fats.</li>
