Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Health Watch ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit health-watch.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our site:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, referral source (via analytics).
  • Tool inputs: Data you enter into our calculators (e.g., height, weight) is processed in your browser and not stored unless you explicitly save it to your dashboard.
  • Dashboard data: Bookmarks, workout logs, nutrition logs, and progress entries you save are stored in our database.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To personalise your dashboard experience
  • To send you newsletter updates (only if you opt in)
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns (anonymised analytics)

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We may use analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand aggregate site usage. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth for sign-in. Your interaction with Google is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Affiliate links on this site may use tracking cookies operated by third-party affiliate networks.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can export or delete your data at any time from your dashboard settings page. Account deletion removes all personally identifiable information from our systems within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us via the contact page.
